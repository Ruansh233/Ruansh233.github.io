## Distances Between POD Subspaces of Repeating Subdomains

Study of local reduced spaces generated from repeating subdomains, including truncation behavior and distances between linear subspaces. This work supports robust local ROM construction in geometries with periodicity and symmetry.

## Simplified Modeling of Wire-Wrapped Bundles

Development and assessment of momentum-source-based simplifications to reduce computational cost while preserving key flow features (pressure drop, mixing trends, and local profile behavior).

![Wire-wrapped bundle geometry and computational domains](/assets/images/research/wire-bundle-msm-geometry.png)

*Figure. Solid domain, fluid domain, cross-section, and wire-region geometry used in the momentum-source-based simplified CFD model for the wire-wrapped bundle study.*

## ROM for Large-Scale Geometries

Research on POD-Galerkin and turbulence-aware reduced models for incompressible flow problems, targeting faster parametric exploration with engineering-relevant accuracy.

![Decomposition of a rod-bundle geometry into local subdomains for reduced-order modeling](/assets/images/research/large-scale-rom-decomposition.png)

*Figure. Decomposition of a rod-bundle geometry into local subdomains to support reduced-order modeling of large-scale parametric flow problems.*

## Parametric Flow Modeling for Wire-Wrapped Bundles

Research on geometric parameterization and reduced-order modeling for wire-wrapped bundle configurations, including free-form deformation and data-driven surrogate approaches to study how geometry variations influence the flow field.

![Parameter-space sampling for wire-wrapped bundle flow modeling](/assets/images/research/wire-bundle-parametric-space.png)

*Figure. Sampled parameter space used for parametric flow modeling of wire-wrapped bundles, illustrating admissible combinations of inlet-profile and fluid-property parameters.*
