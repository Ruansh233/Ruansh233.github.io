## Journal Article

**Ruan, S.; Yanez, J.; Class, A. G.**  
*[Local Reduced Subspaces of Subchannel-inspired Subdomains](https://doi.org/10.1002/nme.7552)*  
International Journal for Numerical Methods in Engineering (2024). 
Keywords: reduced-order modeling, proper orthogonal decomposition, local reduced bases, repeating subdomains, flow blockage  
Topic: Construction and comparison of local POD subspaces for repeating subdomains, with emphasis on truncation behavior, subspace distances, and robust reduced modeling for blockage-sensitive flow configurations.

## Manuscript / Review

**Ruan, S.; Class, A. G.; Rozza, G.**  
*[A Structured Review of Reduced Order Modeling for Domain Decomposition Problems: State of the Art and Perspectives](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09623)*  
Manuscript (2026 revision). 
Keywords: reduced-order modeling, domain decomposition, intrusive methods, non-intrusive methods, coupling strategies, review  
Topic: A structured review of methods that combine reduced-order models with domain decomposition, covering projection-based and data-driven approaches, interface coupling techniques, and open challenges for engineering-scale applications.

## Conference Paper

**Ruan, S.; Otic, I.; Class, A. G.**  
*Simplified Modeling of a Wire-wrapped Bundle Employing a Momentum Source Method*  
Keywords: wire-wrapped bundle, momentum source method, CFD simplification, pressure drop, mixing  
Topic: Development of a simplified CFD methodology for wire-wrapped bundles using anisotropic momentum source terms to reduce computational cost while preserving key hydraulic and mixing characteristics.
