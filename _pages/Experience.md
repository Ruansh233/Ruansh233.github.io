## Doctoral Researcher
**Institute for Thermal Energy Technology and Safety (ITES), Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)**  
Focus: CFD, reduced-order modeling, and machine-learning-enhanced simulation methods for thermal-hydraulics and reactor bundle flow analysis.

### Main Responsibilities

- Conduct high-fidelity CFD simulations for parameterized flow scenarios.
- Develop ROM workflows based on POD and projection/data-driven techniques.
- Explore machine learning, neural networks, PINNs, and neural operator concepts for physics-aware surrogate modeling.
- Investigate local reduced subspace behavior in repeating and obstructed subdomains.
- Present results in conferences, reviews, and research meetings.

### Research Directions

- Coupling strategies between local reduced models and global flow behavior
- Data-driven and neural-network-based acceleration of simulation workflows
- Acceleration of simulation pipelines for engineering design and safety analysis
- Practical simplification methods for wire-wrapped bundle modeling
